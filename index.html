Tone

Privacy Policy

Last updated 6 July 2026

The short version

Tone does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything the app does happens on your device.

No data collection

Tone has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. The app makes no network requests — no information about you or your usage ever leaves your device.

Your photos

Tone can extract colour palettes from photos you choose. When you use this feature:

  • You select the photo yourself via the system photo picker — Tone never browses your library.
  • All colour analysis happens entirely on your device. Photos are never uploaded anywhere.
  • If you save a palette made from a photo, a copy of that photo is stored privately inside the app on your device, so you can edit the palette later. Deleting the palette (or the app) deletes it.

If you export a palette image and share it, that image goes only where you send it via the standard iOS share sheet.

Your saved data

Your favourited combinations, custom palettes, and app settings are stored locally on your device. They are never synced to a server. Deleting the app deletes this data.

Children's privacy

Because Tone collects no data at all, it collects no data from children.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of Tone ever changes how data is handled, this policy will be updated here before that version is released.
